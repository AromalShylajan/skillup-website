The Problem

In India, many students complete their education without proper career guidance, leading to unemployment, skill gaps, and confusion about their future.

Our Solution

SKILLUP is a career-guidance mobile application designed for students from 8th standard onwards. It helps them discover their interests and strengths through an aptitude-based quiz and provides personalized career recommendations.

How It Works

  • Take an interest & aptitude quiz
  • Get personalized career suggestions
  • Follow a guided skill development path

Key Features

  • Personalized career guidance
  • Skill roadmap and learning paths
  • Industry and job insights
  • Early decision-making support for students

Who Can Use SKILLUP?

Students from 8th grade and above, as well as parents who want to guide their children toward the right career path.
